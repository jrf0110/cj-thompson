About CJ Thompson

Jazz guitarist, composer, singer, and multi-instrumentalist from Dallas, TX
-

His training and experience in various types of music have allowed him to transcend genre barriers while maintaining his unique style and voice. This rare blend of range and individuality will be highlighted on his debut album, set to release in Fall of 2014.

C.J. performs in various groups and capacities throughout DFW, as well as performing regularly as a soloist. He has been featured as a soloist at The Elmhurst Jazz Festival and The Greater St. Louis Jazz Festival, and he was recognized as "Outstanding Jazz Guitarist" in both large and small ensemble categories at the latter. In addition to his demanding performance schedule, C.J. maintains a teaching studio of 40+ students and directs 3 bands at a local music school.

Services Offered



  • Solo Entertainment

    Available for hire as a solo entertainer. Using his voice, guitar, and keyboard, C.J. can provide the perfect music for your event or venue.

  • Band

    For larger events and venues, C.J. can bring a band. Proficient in most contemporary styles, his band of highly trained and experienced musicians will certainly fit your needs.

  • Freelance

    Need a guitarist for your performance or recording project? C.J. is always open to freelance opportunities on any of his instruments.

  • Lessons

    Available for private and group lessons for all ages and skill-levels.

  • Transcription

    C.J. will transcribe and create charts for songs and musical pieces of all styles for all instruments.
